Questions

Common questions about the Pro ecommerce build.

If you’re not sure whether you’re “big enough” for Pro yet, we’d rather give you an honest answer than oversell it.

Do you only work with certain platforms?

For ecommerce we mainly work with Shopify and WooCommerce, but we’re open to chatting if you’re on something else. The important part is that your platform can support clean collections, product data, and tracking.

Can you migrate us from our current store?

Yes, for Pro-level projects we can scope a migration plan (products, customers, and URLs where possible) so you don’t lose history. We’ll flag where things might change for SEO or tracking.

What do we need ready before we start?

A product list (even if it’s in a spreadsheet), your current platform details, brand basics (logo, colours), and a rough idea of your main collections and hero ranges.

Do you manage ads or just build the store?

We focus on the store and the tracking that supports ads. We’re happy to work with your existing marketing team or specialist so everything lines up for them.
