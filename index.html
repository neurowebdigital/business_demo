Questions

Common questions about the Business fitness build.

If you’re not sure whether you’re “big enough” for Business yet, we’d rather give you an honest answer than oversell it.

Do I need an app, or is a website enough?

Many studios use a separate coaching app. The Business Plan focuses on the marketing and lead-gen site that explains your programs and feeds that app.

What content do we need before starting?

Program descriptions, rough pricing, coach bios, a few testimonials, and any good photos or brand assets. We’ll guide you through gaps.

Can this work if we’re mostly online coaching?

Yes. The layout is flexible enough for fully online coaching brands – we simply adapt sections to highlight check-ins, app features, and remote processes.

Do you manage ads, or just build the site?

We focus on the site and the tracking that supports ads. We’re happy to work alongside your existing marketing specialist from day one.
